Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 9, 2025

FLP AcademyWorks ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, trading preferences, and experience level
  • Payment Information: Billing details for premium subscriptions (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • OAuth Information: When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your user ID and optionally your email address and name

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, trading signals viewed, courses accessed, time spent in app
  • Location Data: General location (country/region) for content personalization
  • Push Notification Tokens: Device tokens for sending trading signal alerts and app notifications

1.3 Trading and Educational Data

  • Trading signals you view, bookmark, or interact with
  • Course progress and completion status
  • Community posts, comments, and interactions
  • Performance analytics and engagement metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Services: Deliver trading signals, educational content, and community features
  • Account Management: Create and manage your account, authenticate users
  • Notifications: Send push notifications for trading signals, course updates, and important announcements
  • Personalization: Customize content based on your preferences and experience level
  • Analytics: Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries and provide technical assistance
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us operate our services (hosting, analytics, payment processing)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share information

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • JWT token-based authentication
  • Keychain storage for sensitive credentials on iOS devices
  • Regular security audits and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Sign in with Apple

When you use Sign in with Apple:

  • We receive a unique Apple user ID and optionally your email address
  • You can choose to hide your email, in which case Apple provides a private relay email
  • We link this information to your FLP AcademyWorks account
  • You can manage your Sign in with Apple settings in your Apple ID account settings

6. Push Notifications

We use push notifications to send:

  • Real-time trading signal alerts
  • Course updates and new content notifications
  • Community activity (replies, mentions, likes)
  • Account and subscription updates

You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings or app settings.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where we must retain it for legal compliance.

8. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@flpacademyworks.com

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Sending a push notification or email
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

12. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party services (trading platforms, payment processors). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

14. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

European Economic Area (EEA) residents have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Legal basis for processing (consent, contract, legitimate interest)
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to restriction of processing
